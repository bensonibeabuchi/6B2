Favourite thing 1

Photojournalism

Photojournalism is the communication of news through photographs. These images tend to appear in print newspapers and magazines as well as online media sites and microblogging platforms.

A picture is worth 1,000 words and can instantly enhance the story that a news article communicates to its readers.
